# Purpose

This document captures future product ideas that are intentionally outside the current MVP.

Adding an idea to this document does **not** mean it has been approved for development.

Features should only move into active development after they are validated, prioritized, and incorporated into the Product Requirements document.

---

# Guiding Principle

The MVP should remain focused.

Ideas are easy to add.

Maintaining a focused product is much harder.

When considering a future feature, ask:

> **Does this deepen the relationship between people and the music they physically own?**

If not, reconsider whether it belongs in Crates.

---

# Next

These ideas are expected to be among the first considered after the MVP.

## Custom Releases

Allow users to create releases that do not exist in Discogs or other metadata providers.

Examples include:

- Independent artists
- Self-released albums
- Demo tapes
- Bootlegs
- Test pressings
- Personal recordings

**Important:** The application architecture should support custom releases from the beginning, even though the feature is deferred.

---

## Album Cover Recognition

Allow users to identify music by photographing the front cover.

Potential implementations may include AI vision services or third-party image recognition APIs.

---

## Smart Home

Expand the Home experience with more personalized content, including:

- Forgotten favorites
- Seasonal listening
- Collection milestones
- Listening streaks

---

## Enhanced Artist Pages

Artist pages should eventually become rich exploration hubs with:

- Collection statistics
- Personal favorites
- Listening history
- Related artists
- Additional metadata

# Later

These features become valuable once the core experience is mature.

## Rich Track Metadata

Support information such as:

- BPM
- Musical key
- Mood
- Energy
- Instrumentation

---

## Journal Enhancements

Support richer Journal entries, including:

- Photos
- Voice memos
- Cleaning history
- Equipment notes
- Setlists

---

## Additional Physical Media

Expand beyond vinyl to support:

- CDs
- Cassettes
- MiniDiscs
- Reel-to-reel
- Additional collectible music formats

---

## Collection Insights

Automatically surface meaningful statistics, such as:

- Most played artists
- Favorite genres
- Collection growth
- Listening habits

---

## Collection Value

Estimate collection value using external pricing data.

Track value changes over time.
